Eco-friendly ⩟

DAYLY
ECO-TIPS

Use reusable bags instead of plastic when shopping.
Turn off lights & fans when not in use.
Carry a reusable water bottle to avoid buying plastic ones.
Walk or cycle short distances instead of using vehicles.
Unplug chargers & electronics when not in use.
Say no to single-use plastic (spoons, straws, cups).
Plant one small plant in your home or balcony.
Take shorter showers to save water.
Segregate your waste into dry and wet bins.
Carry your own coffee/tea cup when you go out.
